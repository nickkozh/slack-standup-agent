Features

🔍 Intelligent Parsing

Claude Sonnet analyzes standup messages with contextual understanding. Distinguishes real blockers from status updates, assigns urgency levels, and categorizes by type.

📧 Instant Email Alerts

Affected team members get styled HTML emails via Inkbox within seconds. Includes blocker details, urgency, category, and the action they need to take.

🔐 Vault History

Every blocker is stored in Inkbox Vault with full metadata. Daily indices enable time-based queries and resolution tracking.

🚨 Priority Escalation

Unresolved blockers older than your threshold (default: 24h) automatically escalate to the team lead with a summary table.

💬 Thread Replies

The agent replies directly in the Slack thread with detected blockers, who was notified, and any escalations — immediate visibility for the whole team.

🏷 Smart Categorization

Blockers are categorized as design, engineering, devops, review, decision, or external — making it easy to filter and route.
